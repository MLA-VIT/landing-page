About Us

Malayalam Literary Association (Thanima-MLA) is a literary club, by the Keralites of Vellore Institute of Technology. We are a club who thrives to preserve, promote, and uplift the rich, old traditions, cultures, and language of Kerala. In MLA, we help our members to develop their knowledge more on their mother tongue, Malayalam, by conducting weekly events, wherein each and every member have a chance to learn, interact, showcase their literary skills and converse with their peers. We promote Malayalam literature to help other students interested in Malayalam history and literature through these weekly events, be it debates on Malayalam literature, Just a Minute, literary crosswords, Turn Coats and many more, we train our members for competitions and fests. Through so, we all develop a strong bond amongst each other, making MLA, not just a club, but also a family.
